PASSION MEETS DISCIPLINE

I'm Nilesh Kumar — a certified personal trainer based in Bengaluru with over a decade of hands-on experience helping everyday people achieve extraordinary physical transformations.

My philosophy is simple: the best gym is one you'll actually use. That's why I bring professional-grade training directly to your home, designing workouts around your space, your body, and your goals.

From busy professionals and new mothers to retirees and athletes — I've worked with all types. Each program I create is 100% personalised, science-backed, and built for sustainable, long-term results.
